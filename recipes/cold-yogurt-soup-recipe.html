prep time:
10mins
servings:
4

skill level:
Easy
likes:
41

Ingredients

  • 1 Large English cucumber (peeled and finely diced)
  • 1 Garlic clove , pressed
  • ½ tsp Salt
  • 2 Tbsp Olive or sunflower oil (optional)
  • 2-3 Tbsp Chopped fresh dill
  • 2 cups Plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 cups Cold water
  • 1 bunch dill
  • 3 large red beets

Directions

  • 1 In a large deep bowl combine cucumber, garlic, salt, oil and dill.
  • 2 Add yogurt and stir to combine. Dilute with cold water, mixing well, making sure there are no lumps from the yogurt. Add more salt if needed.
  • 3 Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, this way the soup is even more refreshing.
